About me
个人简历
I obtain my B.S degree in 2024 from the School of Computer Science, Nanjing university of Posts and telecommunications (NJUPT), where I am currently pursuing a M.S degree. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Guozi Sun. My past research interests include blockchain, federated learning and data market.
